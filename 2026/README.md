# 2026 State of OSPOs and Open Source Management - Planning

TODO and LF Research are kicking off the 2026 State of OSPOs and Open Source Management study, focused on how emerging technologies and cultural shifts are influencing open source management in organizations and the role of OSPOs. 


## Methodology

Survey fielded in LF and TODO community groups with supporting Open Source Management case studies

## Call for Sponsors

At a high level, sponsorship helps cover:

- Survey design, fielding, and respondent sampling
- Data segmentation and analysis
- Report production
- Promotion of findings

For further details please see the [sponsorship prospectus](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/26208472/TODO_.2026.Call.for.Sponsors-1.pdf)

## Call for Community Feedback

TODO General members, associates, working group maintainers, and ambassadors are welcome to contribute to the 2026 edition by:

- Sharing suggestions and feedback on the draft survey instrument
- Participating in a short interview to be quoted in the final report as a supporting case study


## Timeline

- May and June: survey development, launch, fielding
- July to September: data analysis, report drafting, production
- October: publication at Open Source Summit Europe
