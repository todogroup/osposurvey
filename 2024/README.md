# OSPO Survey 2024 Planning

Interested contributors are invited to submit their suggestions on the 2024 questionnaire by opening an [Issue](https://github.com/todogroup/osposurvey/issues).

## Timeline:

- ✅ January - March: Call for Sponsors
- 🕐 April: Request for Community Feedback and Partnerships for Distribution
- May: Finalize Questionnaire in Multiple Languages and Begin Distribution Campaign
- June - July: Collect Data
- August: Analyze Data and Prepare Report
- September: Launch Survey Report and Communication Campaign
